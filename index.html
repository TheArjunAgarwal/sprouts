GRABBING CARDS!
🃏

Twenty-six cards laid between you. Take from either end, one at a time. The higher sum wins.

THE RULES

  • 26 cards are dealt faceup in a line
  • Players alternate picking from either end
  • Card values: Ace = 1, Numbers = face, J = 11, Q = 12, K = 13
  • Highest total when cards run out wins
